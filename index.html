Quality work in Noroff Vocational School

Quality work in Noroff Vocational School will contribute to our students achieving a learning outcome of high quality and relevance in today’s and tomorrow`s work life.The aim of Noroff Vocational School’s quality system is to ensure a systematic approach in evaluating and further developing our educational programs.

Noroff Vocational School`s objective is to be characterized by “culture for quality”, and for the quality system to facilitate and stimulate good processes in working with educational quality. Targeted, systematic, and continuous quality work contributes to ensuring quality development in our educational programs, and involves staff and students as well as external partners from the industry.
